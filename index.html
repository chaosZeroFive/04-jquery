Crystal Collector!

After clicking Let's Play You will be given a random number.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, but you lose if your total score goes above the Game Number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it...So be sure to remember what the value was.

Each time the game starts...the game will change the value of each crystal.

Earn rewards after two wins
